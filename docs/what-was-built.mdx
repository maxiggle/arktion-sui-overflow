## Authentication & Identity

- **Google OAuth with invisible Sui wallet** via zkLogin — no seed phrase, no browser extension, no crypto knowledge required.
- **ArktionPassport** minted on first login — soul-bound NFT accumulating reading history, INK balance, badges, and level; non-transferable at the Move type level.
- **Six reader levels** (Wanderer → Arktion Elder) based on INK earned, not spent — no pay-to-win.

## Reading

- **Automatic progress tracking** — progress is recorded as you read. Finishing a chapter (scroll-to-end) advances your position, and finishing the last chapter of a completed series marks it done — no manual "mark as read." Progress only ever moves forward, so re-reading never regresses your history.
- **Reading library** — every series you read is tracked with statuses: Reading, Completed, On-Hold, Dropped, Plan to Read.
- **Format-aware reader** — vertical scroll for manhwa/webtoon, paginated for manga/manhua, markdown renderer for novels.
- **Pluggable content sources** — series are served through a `ContentSourceAdapter` abstraction; MangaDex (10,000+ series) is the first source, and creator-uploaded chapters are served the same way.
- **External journal** — track series on other platforms stored on-chain for portability.
- **Reading history page** — full log of chapters read across all series.

## Economy & Community

- **INK token** — earned through reading, never purchasable; minted on Sui via `ink_earning` with idempotency keys preventing double-minting. Rates: 10 INK per chapter milestone, 100 INK for completing a series, 50 INK when a community submission you made gets approved by the DAO. See [INK System](/ink-system) for full details.
- **Six reader levels** — Wanderer (0 INK) → Seeker (500) → Devoted (2,000) → Lorekeeper (6,000) → Chronicle (15,000) → Arktion Elder (40,000). Level is calculated from _lifetime_ INK earned, not current balance.
- **USDC tipping** — direct to creator wallets on Sui. USDC transfers are free on Sui, ~400ms confirmation.
- **Soul-bound badge system** — reading achievements, community badges, series credentials; minted on-chain, non-transferable.
- **Community series submissions** — any reader can suggest a series for the platform; approved submissions earn the submitter 50 INK + a Contributor badge, minted atomically in a single sponsored PTB.
- **DAO voting on submissions** — INK-weighted community governance.
  - Eligibility: must hold at least 1 INK (must have read at least one chapter).
  - Vote weight: current INK balance at cast time.
  - Quorum: 500 total INK weight across all votes.
  - Approval threshold: 60%.
  - Voting window: 7 days. Auto-finalises when quorum and a decisive margin are both met.
  - Admin emergency override retained for spam or abuse cases.

## Creator Tools

- **Creator application flow** — creators apply from within the reader app; application status tracked in Postgres.
- **Creator portal** — publish series, manage chapters, view earnings.
- **Novel chapter editor** — split-pane markdown editor with live preview, word count, and read-time estimate; content uploaded to Walrus.
- **Image chapter upload** — drag-and-drop page upload for manga/manhwa formats, uploaded to Walrus.
- **AI Writing Assistant** — context-aware writing panel embedded in the chapter editor. See [AI Writing Assistant](/ai-writing-assistant).

## Admin

- **Admin dashboard** — three ranked roles (Super Admin, Moderator, Reviewer) with mandatory TOTP, audit-logged actions, user management, content reports, and on-chain contract monitoring.
- **Hardened admin auth** — bcrypt password hashing, login lockout after repeated failures, AES-256-GCM-encrypted TOTP secrets, single-use backup codes, and stateful refresh-token sessions that can be revoked instantly.
- **Four-eyes principle** — a role change is a two-step request/approve flow: one admin proposes it, a *different* admin must approve before it takes effect. Every step is audit-logged.
- See [Security & Trust](/security) for the full auth, session, and transaction-integrity model.

## Infrastructure

- **8 Move smart contracts** deployed on Sui testnet (`ink`, `ink_earning`, `passport`, `reading_history`, `journal`, `submission`, `badges`, `admin`).
- **NestJS backend** — sole point of contact with Sui; constructs, sponsors, and submits every transaction.
- **Gas sponsorship** — every user transaction is wrapped in a sponsored PTB; users never need SUI.
- **Walrus storage** — novel chapter content, badge metadata, and reading history snapshots stored as content-addressed blobs.
