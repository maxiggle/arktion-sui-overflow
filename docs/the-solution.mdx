Arktion replaces the platform middleman with Sui smart contracts.

## How It Works

**Creators own their audience wallets directly** — no platform can revoke that. A creator's subscribers are identified by their Sui wallet addresses, not by a platform-controlled database row that can be deleted overnight.

**Earnings flow peer-to-peer in USDC on Sui**, gas-sponsored so users never need to hold SUI. A tip goes from the reader's wallet to the creator's wallet. There is no escrow, no platform cut, no payment processor ban risk.

**Readers build a portable on-chain reading identity** through the ArktionPassport — an NFT that accumulates history and levels across every series they read. It is not locked to Arktion. It lives on Sui, visible to any app that knows how to read it.

**The INK token, earned only through reading**, gives the community real governance weight over what content gets added without letting anyone buy their way in. One INK = one unit of reading activity. A brand-new account that has never opened a chapter cannot vote.

## The Three Guarantees

<Steps>
  <Step title="Censorship resistance">
    Smart contracts are not accounts. They cannot be banned. Arktion's servers can go offline and a creator's wallet still holds their audience relationships and earnings history on Sui.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permanent ownership">
    The ArktionPassport soul-bound NFT is non-transferable at the Move type level — not as a policy check. A reader's identity and level cannot be taken from them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earned, not bought">
    INK cannot be purchased. It can only be earned by reading. This ensures governance weight reflects genuine platform engagement, not financial power.
  </Step>
</Steps>
